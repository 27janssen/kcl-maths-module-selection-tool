About This Project KCL Maths Hub
When I was picking my modules for my second year at King's College London, I felt as if KEATS had a massive lack of information for me and how much my module selections really mattered.

I never had any intent to publish this website and it's tools as it was just to see if I could make a clear way to communicate information which the Mathematics Department provides, what I believe this project highlights is the need for KEATS to be overhauled and made more accessible for the student body.
The Creator Student
Sebastian Janssen
BSc Mathematics & Philosophy · Year 1 · King's College London

As of writing this, I've been voted in as President of the Mathematics Society at KCL for next year, I have aspirations to potentially share this project to help next year's first and second years make more informed choices about their modules.
